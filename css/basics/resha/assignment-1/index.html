Himalayan Mountains

The Himalayan Mountains are one of the youngest and highest mountain ranges in the world, stretching across five countries: India, Nepal, Bhutan, China, and Pakistan. They were formed by the collision of the Indian and Eurasian tectonic plates. Home to Mount Everest, the highest peak on Earth, the Himalayas are known for their breathtaking beauty, snow-covered peaks, and diverse ecosystems.

  • Mount Everest - the tallest peak.
  • Snow leopards and red pandas.
  • Spiritual destinations like ladakh & lumbini.
