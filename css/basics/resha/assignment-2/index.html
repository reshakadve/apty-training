Chocolate Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas and mix with melted butter.
  3. Add sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Stir in the flour and cocoa powder until just combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  6. Pour into the loaf pan and bake for 55-60 minutes.
  7. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Rich, moist, and loaded with chocolatey banana goodness!
